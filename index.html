    Our Mission


    • Develop the skills and confidence of educators by the use of appropriate and effective use of digital technology to support learning and teaching.

    • Ensure that digital technology is a central consideration in all areas of curriculum and assessment delivery.
    • To Facilitate students academically by providing them with the 21st Century learning tool.

    • Enhance the overall college experience at PVGCOEN, generating enthusiasm and a more positive learning environment for students.
